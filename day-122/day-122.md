Welcome to **day 122** of 365 days of code - coding every day for a year, little and often

Now I'll be honest, DB's have never been something that I've known alot about, other than querying them, so I did a bit of looking into them, and then by extension relations in Drizzle.

Tomorrow, the plan is to take a look through the DB queries and see if there are any that can take advantage of the relations.
