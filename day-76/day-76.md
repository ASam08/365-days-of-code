#  Little And Often
Welcome to **day 76** of 365 days of code - coding every day for a year, little and often

Really not a big day at all today, but every day, little and often is the goal and therefore I have achieved.

While I was thinking about how best to implement client side validation on the add timetable block form, I realised that using state was probably better to hold the start and end time in, rather than querying the DOM as I did on the settings page. With the limited time I had today, I thought it would be best to test the theory on the settings page, as that's the only client side validation, rather than spend all the time building out the add-block validation only to realise it didn't work, so that's what I did.

I'm glad I did, but not because it didn't work (it did), it's just really all that I could find time for today. I know making the change to the settings page shouldn't be on this branch because it's a feature branch specifically for the day of week settings, but I did, so...
