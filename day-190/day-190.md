#  Pnpm Lock
Welcome to **day 190** of 365 days of code - coding every day for a year, little and often

Honestly today was one of those weird days. I had a dependabot update which was failing both the build test and the jest test, but then when I pulled the branch locally, it was passing both. I thought it was just a case of push that back up, and things would be ok, but that didn't make a difference either.

So of course after a bit of mucking around trying to work out the issue, I finally read the errors properly and realised that the lockfile needed to be cleaned. Sure enough, "pnpm clean --lockfile" and "pnpm install", and we were back in business, passing everything and away and laughing.

After all the messing around trying to work that out, I ran out of time to do anything else today, so whilst it's not glamorous, that was me today...more tomorrow!
