#  Little Changes
Welcome to **day 74** of 365 days of code - coding every day for a year, little and often

Another step in the right direction today, a bit more energy and able to tackle the shift to toggle the alert dialog for the hidden day of week via the Save Changes button now, and only when the selected day is hidden.

If someone is submitting a form and they have selected the day of week but missed something else, and that day of week is hidden, they'll get the pop-up, and then get a error afterwards, not a great user experience. Not fully part of the day of week settings, but related.
