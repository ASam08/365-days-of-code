Well after yesterday's difficulties, today was surprisingly good. I ended up going back to the drawing board completely on the addtimetableblock tests, and it's working. I had some fair assistance from Claude, but one of the big changes was actually something I caused to break when I started moving some of my functions into lib/utils. Well, once I worked that out, we were off to the races so to speak, and I've now got the addtimetableblock (and setttings as I'd broken that too) fully covered from a testing perspective. All in all a good day, and a good learn.
