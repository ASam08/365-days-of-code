#  Central Mocks - Part 2
Welcome to **day 174** of 365 days of code - coding every day for a year, little and often

Not a bad day today, not a whole lot of time, but I did manage to knock out 2 central mocks, Input and Field. Admittedly neither of these are particularly hard mocks to put together, and they don't have a whole bunch of configuration to them, but good to start with the low hanging fruit, especially when I am short on time.

I know this seems a little boring, and in some ways it is, but there's enough in there to keep it interesting for me for now, and to be fair, sometimes writing code, and maintaining a codebase *is boring*, that's not exactly a newsflash if you do it all the time, but I guess it might surprise some. I also know I could just get AI to do this for me, it's sort of donkey work, but I'm cautious of just letting AI do easy work, if we always get it to do the easy work, then we won't really know how the easy stuff works...

Anyway that's that for today, I guess that means more tomorrow!
